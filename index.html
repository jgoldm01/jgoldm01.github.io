ABOUT ME
Having graduated from Tufts University a semester early with a double major in Computer Science and Economics, I find myself well versed in the arts of critical thinking and problem solving. My focus is back-end development across the tech stack, with expertise in algorithm development and systems integration. I also design user interfaces for web and software applications, and dabble in computer security. I look forward to a life of building interesting products and working on new, experimental technologies.

When I am not being a 'screen wizard' - my euphemistic term for a software engineer - you will often find me on my bicycle. I am also a committed musician. My favorite musical activity is sight reading the piano, yet I am currently teaching myself the guitar and exploring music production software.
